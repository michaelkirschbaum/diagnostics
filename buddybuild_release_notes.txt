WHAT'S NEW IN VERSION X.XX
--------------------------

iOS SDK:
 - use of backoff timer on straight line drive event data colection, initially 1 second, maximum 512 seconds, each drive tye has separate timer
 - loosened tolerances for straight line drive events:
   + SLS is +/- 4 mps speed and +/- 5 degrees for bearing
   + SLA/SLD +/- 0.5 mps speed and +/- 5 degrees for bearing
